Step 2: Drop Food & Collect Bacteria

We dropped food onto the floor for under 5 seconds to test the “5-second rule.”

After picking the food up, we used a sterile swab to collect any bacteria that may have transferred from the surface. The swab was then used to inoculate our agar plates so we could observe bacterial growth.

This step helps us determine whether bacteria can transfer instantly, even with very short exposure times.
